About Us


The Titi-Laoks is a charming chicken store that began as a humble dream in the small town of Featherdale. Founded by the determined entrepreneur Downtown Balagbag Jr. our story unfolds from modest beginnings to a thriving poultry empire. Starting with fresh eggs and free-range chickens, Balagbag's commitment to quality, creativity, and community engagement propelled the store's success. Today, Titi-Laoks stands as a beloved brand with multiple branches nationwide, offering a delightful array of gourmet chicken dishes and exclusive chicken-themed merchandise. Join us on this extraordinary journey from a small backyard flock to a nationwide sensation, celebrating the values of perseverance, sustainability, and locally sourced excellence.
